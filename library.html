The Scan & Go Workflow

1. Scan Book

Use any standard barcode scanner to scan the book's unique code. The system instantly fetches the book details.

2. Scan Student ID

Scan the student's ID card. The system checks if they have reached their max book limit or have pending fines.

3. Issue & Print

Click "Issue". The inventory is updated, and an optional issue slip is printed or emailed to the student.

OPAC Search

Online Public Access Catalog. Students can search for books from their mobile app to see if they are available on the shelf.

Book Bank

Manage semester-long issues. Issue a set of 5-6 books to a student for the entire semester in one click.

Stock Verification

End-of-year audit made easy. Generate reports on lost, damaged, or written-off books effortlessly.
