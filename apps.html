Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before launching.

Why do you need an App?

Browsers are great for searching, but Apps are built for learning. Increase your student retention.

Are there any hidden costs?

ERPLI charges ₹999/year flat. The only other cost is the one-time Google Play Console fee ($25 approx ₹2,100) which you pay directly to Google to create your developer account.

Who owns the App?

You do. The app is published under your Developer Account. Even if you leave ERPLI in the future, your student data and app listing remain yours.

Is video hosting included?

Yes, we provide secure hosting. We have a fair usage policy (FUP) that covers 99% of educators. For extremely high usage (10k+ students), we offer enterprise bandwidth add-ons.

What about the iOS App?

We provide an iOS-compatible web app experience that students can add to their home screen. A native iOS app is available on our 'Scale' plan.
