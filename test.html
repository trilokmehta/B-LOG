Common Questions

What's the catch with ₹999?
No catch. We use enterprise cloud architecture to keep costs low and pass those savings to you.
Can I use my own domain?
Yes! Connect your .com or .in domain easily in one click.
Do you take any commission on my course sales?
Absolutely not. Unlike other platforms that take 10-30%, ERPLI is a 0% commission platform. Your hard-earned money stays with you.
Is the ₹999 price per student or per year?
It is a flat fee of ₹999 per year for the basic ERP suite. There are no "per-student" charges, so you can grow from 10 to 10,000 students without worrying about rising costs.
How do you prevent students from sharing my videos?
We use multi-layer protection: Device-locked logins (one account = one device), dynamic watermarking (student's email/phone floats on screen), and anti-screen record technology.
Can students download videos for offline viewing?
Yes, but only within the encrypted ERPLI app. They cannot access the raw file in their gallery or share it via WhatsApp or Telegram.
Can I manage my offline coaching staff with this?
Yes. You can create sub-admin roles for your receptionists, teachers, and accountants with restricted access to specific data like fee collection or attendance.
