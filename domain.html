Common Questions

Do you sell domains?

No, we don't sell domains directly. You buy it from any registrar (GoDaddy, etc.) and we simply connect it to your site.

Is the SSL certificate free?

Yes! We automatically provision a secure HTTPS certificate for your custom domain at no extra cost.

Can I use a subdomain?

Yes, you can use something like courses.myschool.com if you already have a main website.

How long does it take?

Once you add the CNAME record, it usually propagates worldwide within 10 to 30 minutes.
