Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Info: Name, email address, phone number, and password.
  • Business Info: Institute name, logo, and course details.
  • Student Data: Information you upload about your students (names, attendance logs, grades).

2. Data Ownership Guarantee

We Do Not Sell Your Data:

ERPLI acknowledges that your student database is your proprietary asset. We do not sell, rent, or share your student's contact details with third-party advertisers or other institutes.

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected data strictly to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the ERPLI platform.
  • Process transactions and send related information (invoices, confirmations).
  • Send administrative information (updates, security alerts).
  • Provide customer support.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including SSL encryption and secure server infrastructure, to protect your personal information and your student data from unauthorized access.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience on our site, such as keeping you logged in and understanding how you use our dashboard.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party providers (e.g., Razorpay/Stripe for payments, AWS for hosting). These parties have access to your data only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@erpli.com.
