Common Questions

Do I need a Zoom License?

Yes, you need your own Zoom Pro account to host classes longer than 40 minutes. We simply integrate it.

Does it work with the Free Plan?

You can use Google Meet (free) or Zoom Free (40 min limit) with our platform seamlessly.

Where are recordings stored?

Recordings are moved from Zoom Cloud to ERPLI's secure storage automatically, saving you Zoom storage fees.

Can I restrict access?

Absolutely. Only students enrolled in that specific batch can see the "Join" button.
